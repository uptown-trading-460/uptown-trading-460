WHO WE ARE

Executive Summary

Uptown Trading 460 CC is a proudly South African, 100% Black-Owned Close Corporation that has been in business since 02 February 2005 — giving the company nearly two decades of proven trading history.

Originally registered for "General Trading in All Aspects," the company evolved through formal CIPC amendments in 2013 and 2016 to specialize in Mining Industry Supply, Construction, Building Maintenance, Renovations, Repair, Transport, Supply, Sewing, and Landscaping.

We supply mining equipment, lubricants, PPE, consumables, batteries, tyres, and general industrial supply solutions to the mining sector — proudly serving major operations including Bathopele Platinum Mine, Waterval Chrome Mine (Cybo), and Aquarius Platinum (SA) Pty Ltd.

Under the leadership of Mr. Collen Louis Maraba, who took 100% ownership on 27 October 2015, Uptown Trading 460 CC has successfully delivered services to numerous Government Departments including Public Works, SAPS, Correctional Services, the Department of Higher Education & Training, SASSA, Mogale City Local Municipality, and the South African National Defence Force.

The company is fully tender-ready, registered on the National Treasury Central Supplier Database (CSD), and banks with First National Bank (FNB).
